If You're Reading This...

It means this message found you. It's funny how life works. A whole year of talking, then a year of silence, and a move to another state. It feels like a lifetime ago. With your birthday coming up, I couldn't help but remember all our conversations.

I know things changed, and we drifted apart. There's no one to blame; it's just what happens. But I hope you know that our friendship meant a lot to me. It was a bright spot, and I still think about the laughter and the stories we shared. This little corner of the internet is my way of sending a wish out into the universe for you.

I hope your new school is amazing, that you've found wonderful friends, and that you're incredibly happy. Mostly, I hope you have the most amazing birthday.

- Wishing you the best, always.
